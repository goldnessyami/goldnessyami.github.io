About Me

"Hey there, I’m Eva Veronese, Goldnessyami. I’m a model , cosplayer and an artist all arround, working and living in Italy.
I am a freelance professional model specialized in photo shoots in elegant clothes, in bathing suits, underwear, for advertising campaigns, events, catalogs, calendars, photo exhibitions, in cosplaying.
As a professional in the work I use the utmost seriousness, working on the poses, on the make-up, on the rendering of the expections of the face, to get the maximum expression, plasticity and beauty from every single photo shoot.
I work with many photographers trying always to renew outfits, locations and subjects because I think the variety is synonymous with quality and experience in this field.
As an all-round artist I also try my hand at singing, drawing and acting, and I am therefore available to works that go beyond simple photography.
I'm available and I also take part in fashion shows in Italy and abroad, where I get excited and do my best."
